What is a UK Visa?

A UK visa allows you to enter the United Kingdom for tourism, business, studies, family visits, or medical reasons.

One visa – endless experiences across iconic cities, royal history, green countryside, and modern culture.

Types of UK Visas

  • • Tourist Visa (Standard Visitor)
  • • Business Visa
  • • Family Visit Visa
  • • Medical Treatment Visa
  • • Student Visa (Short & Long Term)
  • • Transit Visa

📌 UK Standard Visitor Visas are typically valid for 6 months. Multiple-entry visas up to 2, 5, or 10 years are also available for frequent travelers.

Key Documents Required

  • • Valid passport
  • • UAE residence visa + Emirates ID
  • • No Objection Certificate (NOC) – if employed
  • • Trade Licence – if self-employed
  • • Bank statements – past 6 months
  • • Passport-sized photo – 35×45 mm, white background
  • • Travel plan or invitation letter – depending on purpose
  • • Previous visas (if available)

Special Categories

  • • Children and minors / students
  • • Non-working applicants
  • • Housewives and dependents
  • • Entrepreneurs and investors
  • • Golden Visa holders
  • • Medical cases and seniors

Processing Time

🕒 Most UK visitor visas are processed in 15–20 working days,
but for some nationalities, processing can take up to 1.5 months.

⚡ Priority and Super Priority services are available for faster decisions.

📌 You can also choose the “Keep My Passport” option – allowing you to retain your passport during most of the processing period.
This is especially useful if you have upcoming travel plans or other visa applications.

📌 We recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before your planned travel date.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need confirmed bookings?

❓ Is an interview required?

✔️ Not usually. Most applications are reviewed based on documents.
